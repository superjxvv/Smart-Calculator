Dating App Open on JetBrains Academy

Bryce is looking for a new girlfriend in a dating app. Bryce believes that the cutest girls study at MIT and write something about themselves in the description. Help Bryce and write a program that selects from the girls list those that fit the criteria and displays their names.

Here's an example of the dating app's list:

girls = [
  {"name": "Ashley", "education": "Harvard", "age": 21, "about": "I like drawing circles"},
  {"name": "Daisy", "education": "MIT", "age": 28, "about": ""},
  {"name": "Maria", "education": "MIT", "age": 21, "about": "Adore traveling and Data Science"},
  {"name": "Stacy", "education": "Harvard", "age": 28, "about": ""},
  {"name": "Kate", "education": "Harvard", "age": 21, "about": "Don't write me if your IQ is lower than mine"},
  {"name": "Leyla", "education": "MIT", "age": 19, "about": "Do not write me that the cat is not bread, please"}]

The output, in this case, should be printed as a string with the girls' names separated by a comma and a space: Maria, Leyla.
